Danon Aerial Photography has been delivering breathtaking, professional-grade imagery since 2020. Based in the Dallas-Fort Worth area and actively serving San Diego, we bring a cinematic eye and technical precision to every project.

We specialize in stunning aerial photography using a lightweight sub-250g drone — offering greater flexibility to fly in more locations with fewer restrictions, without sacrificing image quality.
